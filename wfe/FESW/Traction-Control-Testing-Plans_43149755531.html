Firmware : Traction Control Testing Plans

Created by Justin Vuong, last modified on Aug 08, 2022

We will be running a bunch of acceleration events (75-meter course) and timing them to tune the car.

  • It would be good to profile the car to see what kind of maximums we reach for power, torque, and torque ramp rate

  • Torque Ramp Rate, torque limit, power limit are configurable in Gen5MC and kP for traction control is in the code.

    • Torque limit: Starting from a stand still if the wheels are slipping as the car is accelerating reduce the torque limit

    • Torque ramp: start with a high torque ramp rate (we want this as high as possible without slipping)

      • If there is slipping once the car has already started to move, reduce the torque ramp value

    • Power limit:

      • If at low speeds there is not enough toque we can increase the power limit

      • Remember power = torque * speed
