Description

The load switches are what they sound.  They are able to switch up to 4 output rails on/off given a source rail.  We use these to power our boards.  The PDU knows what board to power on in what sequence and uses the load switches to turn them on individually.

The load switches also have current mirrors to mirror a division of the current (to not waste power) to a resistor.  The PDU reads the voltage drop across this resistor to calculate current for each channel
