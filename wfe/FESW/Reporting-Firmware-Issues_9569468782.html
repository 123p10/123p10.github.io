Firmware : Reporting Firmware Issues

Created by Former user, last modified by Owen Brake on Jan 24, 2021

It is important to report all issues you discover with the firmware. Even if you don't plan to fix them immediately (or at all), it helps document known issues.

To report issues you find, report them on the firmware JIRA board.
