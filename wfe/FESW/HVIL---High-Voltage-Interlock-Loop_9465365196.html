Description

The HVIL (High Voltage Interlock Loop) is a safety loop that runs through the connectors on the access plate of the accumulator to the motor controllers.

If you open the motor controllers while they are on then it will break the loop and cut power

When the loop breaks then discharge the motors by throwing a fault onto CAN and switching state machine into fault state.  This forces the contact control manager to discharge
