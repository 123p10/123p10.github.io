Description

The IMD is a board that sits on-top of the BMU

The IMD checks if HV is shorted to ground.  HV is the negative or positive contact coming out of the HV battery.  These cannot be electrically connected to any part of the car (frame, LV Ground, etc.) The only thing the HV battery contacts are connected to is the motor controllers.  If the IMD senses that the HV battery contacts have continuity to the frame (checks the front and rear) then a signal is sent to the BMU.

The IMD has a status pin that toggles at a specific duty cycle and frequency.  By toggling frequency, it toggles different states
