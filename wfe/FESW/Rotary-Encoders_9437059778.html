Progress Outline and Documentation

  • What is a rotary encoder?

    • Rotary or shaft encoder is an electro-mechanical device that convert the angular position/motion of a shaft/axle to an analog or digital signal. Rotary encoders are used in many applications that require precise shaft unlimited rotation (aka continuous rotation).

    • Absolute Encoder: Maintains position information when power is removed for the system - encoder position immediately available when power is applied.

    • Incremental Encoder: Records changes in position but doesn’t power up with a fixed relation between encoder state and physical position. Thus, devices controlled by incremental encoders may need to “go home” to a fixed reference point to initialize position measurement.
