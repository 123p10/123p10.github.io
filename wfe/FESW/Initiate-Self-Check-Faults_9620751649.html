Created by Owen Brake, last modified on Dec 02, 2021

Preliminary

Note: I do not know how possible this is. Can we test triggering things like the IL faults on the BMU easily?

Summary

The boards have various self sanity checks they perform. It would be a good idea to check them all.

Examples:

  • VCU

    • Brake test

      • Validate that pushing brake and throttle together triggers a DTC

      • Validate that physically moving the throttle in any direction like to it's maximum or minimum does not trigger fail and cause the motor to turn off

  • BMU

    • HVIL test

      • break the HVIL loop then start the car, validate that it blocks car startup until HVIL is closed

    • IL BRB(button) test

      • break the IL loop (press the E-stop buttons), validate that it blocks car startup until IL is closed again (button unpressed)

        • Test all the buttons (this test may not be necessary because we are redoing some of the buttons on the car and how it works.)

    • BSPD test

      • This is the same as above, but trigger the BSPD, I'll have to look at at the schematic to see how we should be trigger this

    • HVD test

      • Remove High Voltage Disconnects and ensure the car doesn't start until it validates that they are plugged in
