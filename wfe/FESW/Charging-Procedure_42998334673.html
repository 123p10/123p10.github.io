Firmware : Charging Procedure

Created by Owen Brake, last modified on Mar 27, 2022

  1. Connect BMU harness to the charge cart

  2. Connect fake motor controller adapters onto battery

  3. Connect HVD

  4. Connect orange charging adapter to BMU

  5. Connect large wall adapter to 120/240V wall outlet with a large breaker (preferrably >5A)

  6. Connect small low voltage wall adapter to any outlet.

  7. Connect barrel jack to top of charge cart barrel jack port

  8. Connect laptop to BMU through Sofia board

  9. Run the following commands in the CLI:

    1. forceChargeMode 1

      1. This tells the BMU that we are going to be in charging mode

    2. canStartCharger

      1. This tells the BMU to start the charger CAN interface

    3. hvToggle

      1. Brings BMU up to high voltage

    4. maxChargeCurrent <parameter>

      1. optional step, the default charge current is 5A

    5. startCharge

      1. Begins charging process

  10. The battery will then begin charging and balancing the cells once the lowest cell voltage is 3.5V

  11. To gracefully end charging run the following commands:

    1. stopCharge

      1. Stops charging process

      2. Wait until you recieve an output of like “charging done“ and the fans stop spinning on the BMU

    2. hvToggle

      1. Brings the BMU down from high voltage

  12. Now you can safely cut off low voltage.
