Firmware : Endurance Testing

Created by Owen Brake on Sep 10, 2021

Preliminary

Summary

We should test the car over a long period of time. This could catch rare firmware faults like memory leaks and perhaps mechanical fatigue.

Some example tests:

  • Driving the car at low speed for a very long time and monitoring the condition
  • Performing very high load driving at high speeds to drain the battery and monitoring what happens as the battery level declines
